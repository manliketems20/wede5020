About Us

Meet Amara

Baking as a love language.

The Flour Honey Bakery begin in 2021 the way most great things do quietly and out of pure love. Amara Petersen, a self taught baker with a background in nutrition had spent years perfecting recipes in her Observatory kitchen

What started as a weekend hobby quickly became something undeniable. The pre orders sold out. The waiting list grew. And so with a proving basket in one hand and a vison in the other, Amara turned her passion into a proper business.
